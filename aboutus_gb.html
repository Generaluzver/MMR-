MMR is a professional record company founded in 1990. Works with genres: death, speed, melodic, gothic, ebm, black, viking metal. In 2002, the company became part of Universal Music Group, but works independently.

MMR is a true Finnish TRU record company founded by Antti Kokko from the cult band "Kalmah". Later he was joined by a guitarist Teemu Mäntysaari "Wintersun".
Were not invested a little money for all the equipment for recording, but this that was worth it. Later, the artist, photo model and writer Aino "Nikki" Järvinen (Aino "Nikki" Järvinen) was invited. She became a gloomy metal muse.
Gaahls Wyrd came to their squad only two months later when she saw the real potential of the team.
A the rest is like everyone else - finding young and old groups, creating a nice service, promotion in the world market ...
MMR is a young but already serious company that gives an opportunity to all informals to open their potential and get a full range of services.
